Giovanni Giorgio

The protagonist of the "Golden Wind". He dreams of becoming the head of the Gang Star mafia and joins the Passione team, being the youngest member, becoming the de facto new leader of the group.

Famous for saying: "My name is Giovanni Giorgio, but everybody calls me Giorgio".
